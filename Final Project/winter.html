Why It Matters

ðŸ’ƒ This goes beyond fun ðŸ•º

01 Simply Affordable Produce in season means they are in abundance at the given time. Not only will they taste delicious, they will likely cost you less compared to eating the same produce during other seasons.
02 Nutritiously Diverse Mixing up veggies and fruits that you consume throughout the year guarantees that you are intaking a wide range of vitamins and minerals. Variety is best when it comes to diet!
03 Socially Beneficial Produce in season are more likely coming from local farms. Eating these veggies and fruits means you are supporting these local entities first.
04 Environmentally Sustainable Eating seasonally supports local farming, which automatically means less transportation, less refrigeration, less hot houses, and irradiation of produce.
